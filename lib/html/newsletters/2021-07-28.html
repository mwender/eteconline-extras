Y-12’s Zero Power Physics Reactor Depleted Uranium Recovery Project

Earlier this year, the NNSA Office of Nuclear Material Integration, in cooperation with Y-12, completed the Zero Power Physics Reactor Depleted Uranium Recovery Project. The effort successfully recovered 28 metric tons of high-purity DU blocks previously used in the ZPPR.
