Snail Darter Matures from Notoriety to Success Story

In late summer, a proposal was released to remove the tiny snail darter from the endangered species list of the Endangered Species Act. In 1975, TVA's unfinished Tellico Dam threatened the snail-eating species. TVA has since improved the quality of the water and added fish habitats. If delisted, it will join 52 other species that have been removed from the ESA due to recovery efforts since 1973.

ETEC welcomes Ultrasonic Technology Solutions.
The drying energy sector in North America is responsible for 15% of the country’s energy consumption. Ultrasonic Technology Solutions has developed industrial and commercial ultrasonic dryers that are two times faster and three to five times more efficient than the conventional heat-based drying system. Their products can cut millions of dollars from operational expenses, and significantly increase the throughput of various manufacturing production lines.
