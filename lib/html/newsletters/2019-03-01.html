Governor Lee to present
"State of East Tennessee” address


You are invited to join Governor Lee next Tuesday evening, March 5, at the Clarence Brown Theater on U.T.’s campus where the Governor will present a “State of East Tennessee” address.

State of East Tennessee
Tuesday, March 5, 2019
6:00 p.m. ET
University of Tennessee - Knoxville
Clarence Brown Theatre
1714 Andy Holt Ave.
Knoxville, TN 37996
Event is free and open to the public but tickets are required for entry.

Tickets are available at https://www.tn.gov/governor/sots.
Gov. Lee will be sharing a series of regional addresses across Tennessee following his formal State of the State address to the General Assembly and members of the public on Monday, March 4 at 6 p.m. CT in the House Chamber of the Tennessee General Assembly in Nashville. The joint session will air statewide on public television
