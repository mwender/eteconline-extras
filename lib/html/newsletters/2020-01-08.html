FOTW #1114: There are Currently about 142,000 Public Gasoline Stations in the U.S.

In 1972 there were nearly 290,000 public gasoline stations serving 106 million vehicles nationwide. In 2019 there are only 142,000 stations serving more than 276 million vehicles.

NRC to Issue Early Site Permit for TVA Clinch River Site

The Nuclear Regulatory Commission has authorized the issuance of an Early Site Permit for Tennessee Valley Authority’s Clinch River site near Oak Ridge, Tenn. The permit closes several site related issues, including many environmental impacts, for small modular reactors at the site.
