ETEC welcomes Southeastern Packaging Technologies, LLC as a new member.
Southeastern Packaging Technologies (SPT) is a vertically integrated complete solutions provider for industry-leading flexible packaging products and flexible filling and packaging solutions. SPT works with high volume manufacturers of consumer-packaged goods and wholesale fluids and commodities in order to optimize their production strategies and achieve required volume, ensure target margins are met, and restructure their North American supply chains for flexible packaging production and distribution.
