Masks Required for In-Person Attendees

ETEC chooses to follow recommendations set by the CDC and because ETEC meets in a University of Tennessee facility, we need to follow UT protocol. The University has asked that masks be worn in all classrooms, laboratories, instructional spaces, and at any indoor event. If you plan to attend in person on Friday, please bring your mask.
