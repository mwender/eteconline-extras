New Member

ETEC welcomes IONEX Research Corporation as a new member.
IONEX is a custom metal manufacturer of design/build equipment for the government, nuclear, and mining industries. A small business operating since 1978, IONEX fabricates equipment to exacting standards and tolerances and maintains a Quality Program that meets NQA-1, 10CFR 50, and ASME Section VIII. IONEX's in-house engineering team includes licensed PE's and a design group capable of assembling a full engineering package for fabrication. They provide integrated engineering solutions to their clients.

UT Collaborates with AT&T on 5G Research and Technology

The University of Tennessee, Knoxville, is poised to play a role in how 5G and its successors are utilized, helping to address some of the main concerns about the technology in the process thanks to an agreement with AT&T that will establish a 5G+ presence on campus, including a test bed.

Nuclear Plant in Alabama Now uses 3D-Printed Components

The Tennessee Valley Authority’s Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant Unit 2 in Alabama has become one of the first plants in the world to use 3D-printed fuel assembly brackets. The plant is employing four of these first-of-a-kind devices produced at DOE’s MDF at ORNL.

Seven Startups Graduate from Tennessee’s Energy Mentor Network
