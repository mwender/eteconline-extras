Work Starts to Increase Power of SNS Proton Beam

Construction work has started on a part of a project to double the power of the proton beam in the Spallation Neutron Source at ORNL. It’s the first construction work at the $1.4 billion SNS since 2006.

Working Group Presents Strategy- US Uranium Revival

Establishing a uranium reserve, ending the DOE’s bartering of uranium, and re-evaluating DOE’s excess uranium inventory management policy are among the US Nuclear Fuel Working Group’s recommendations to revive the USA’s nuclear fuel cycle.

ORNL Shows Fast Wireless Charging On UPS Truck

Researchers at DOE's Oak Ridge National Laboratory in late February demonstrated a 20-kilowatt bi-directional wireless charging system installed on a UPS medium-duty, plug-in hybrid electric delivery truck.
