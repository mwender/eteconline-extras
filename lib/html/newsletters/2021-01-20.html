New Member

ETEC welcomes Ultra Safe Nuclear Corporation (USNC) as a new member.
USNC is a U.S. company developing advanced technology to enhance the safety of nuclear power.
USNC is involved in the fabrication, construction, and deployment of small modular nuclear power plants. USNC employs 100+ people in the U.S. and around the world, including Canada, South Africa, and South Korea. The company is headquartered in Seattle, Washington.
