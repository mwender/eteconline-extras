ETEC Trailblazer Jessemae Myers Noritake Passed Away

A good friend and longtime associate of ETEC passed away last week. Jesse Mae Noritake was the core of the old Roane Anderson Economic Council and was the first hire president Jim Campbell made when he joined ETEC over 20 years ago. As Jim Campbell said, "She knew how to make things work, always with a smile."

OR Environ. Mgmt. Prepares Next Batch of Buildings for Demolition

OREM is preparing the next wave of buildings for demolition at the Y-12 National Security Complex as part of a new chapter of cleanup in Oak Ridge. The massive cleanup at Y-12 is happening simultaneously with other cleanup projects underway at ORNL, where crews are addressing 16 inactive research reactor and isotope facilities.
