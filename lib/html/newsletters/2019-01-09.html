New Member

ETEC Welcomes ARS Aleut Remediation, LLC (AAR).
AAR is a wholly owned subsidiary of ARS International, LLC, is certified with the U.S. Small Business Administration as an 8(a) Alaskan Native Corporation and is a certified small disadvantaged business and minority business enterprise. Their capabilities span the environmental services life cycle including sampling and characterization, remediation, site preparation, construction, decommissioning, demolition, waste packing, and disposition.
